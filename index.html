Our Story · His Faithfulness

Eternal Vows: The Unveiling

This 15th renewal is more than an event; it is a holy echo of a promise kept — a tender unveiling of the beauty God has patiently written into our marriage.

The journey of love reflects the mystery and wonder of commitment: how two people begin with dreams and promises — and over time, God gently shapes each moment, revealing a deeper friendship, a stronger covenant, and a more radiant version of "us" than we ever imagined.

On this sacred day, you are invited into our story — into a space filled with heartfelt worship, renewed vows, elegant celebration, and sacred joy. Our prayer is that every couple, every friend, every heart present will be reminded: love renewed is love made new.
